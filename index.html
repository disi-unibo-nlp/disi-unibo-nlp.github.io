DISI UniBo NLP

The DISI UniBo Natural Language Processing Group (DISI UniBo NLP) in Cesena campus, led by prof. Gianluca Moro, includes Ph.D. students and researchers of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Bologna.

Valuable Tasks

Text Summarization, Semantic Parsing, Data-to-Text, Question Answering, Recommendation Systems, Information Retrieval, Representation Learning, etc.

Research Themes

Generative NLP Models (e.g., BART, T5, GPT), Graph Neural Networks, Memory-Enhanced Neural Networks, Retrieval-Enhanced Language Models, Neuro-Symbolic AI, Knowledge Injection, Neural Reasoning, Deep Metric Learning, Vision-Language AI, Explainability, Large Language Models

Open and Closed Domains

Our research moves from General-domain and Commonsense to Medical, Biomedical, and Legal real-world applications, including collaborations with impactful companies and research groups

Research Group Presentation

Our group aims at devising and developing innovative solutions for Natural Language Processing, Understanding, and Generation. We pursue a vision focused on integrating explicit, symbolic knowledge with deep learning.
