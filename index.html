DISI UniBo NLP

The DISI UniBo Natural Language Processing Group (DISI UniBo NLP), led by prof. Gianluca Moro, includes a team of Ph.D. students and researchers who are part of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Bologna.

Valuable Tasks

Text Summarization, Semantic Parsing, Data-to-Text, Question Answering, Recommendation Systems, Information Retrieval, Representation Learning, etc.

Open and Closed Domains

Medical, Biomedical, Bioinformatics Legal and collaborations

Innovative Trends

Explainability, Memory, Graph Neural Networks, Structured Knowledge Integration, Deep Metric Learning, Cross-Modalities

Research Group Presentation

Our group aims at devising and developing innovative solutions for Natural Language Processing, Understanding, and Generation. We pursue a vision focused on integrating explicit, symbolic knowledge with deep learning.
