UniboNLP

The UniBo Natural Language Processing Group at Cesena, led by Prof. Gianluca Moro, comprises researchers from the University of Bologna’s Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Tasks

Chatbots, Document Summarization, Information Extraction/Retrieval, Question Answering, Image Segmentation, Fact-Checking, Classification

Research Topics

Large Language Models, Graph Neural Networks, End-to-end Differentiability, Agents, Deep Metric Learning, Knowledge Injection, Prompt Learning, Explainable AI, Sparse Autoencoders, Neuro-Symbolic AI

Domains

Long-time experience in biomedical, clinical, and legal applications, collaborating with leading companies and research groups
