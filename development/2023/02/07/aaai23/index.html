AAAI 2023

Published: Feb 7, 2023 by

We are proud to announce that our group has two paper accepted at AAAI 23.

  • “Cogito Ergo Summ: Abstractive Summarization of Biomedical Papers via Semantic Parsing Graphs and Consistency Rewards” (Main Track)
  • “Carburacy: Summarization Models Tuning and Comparison in Eco-Sustainable Regimes with a Novel Carbon-Aware Accuracy” (Artificial Intelligence for Social Impact track)

If you are interested check https://github.com/disi-unibo-nlp/cogito-ergo-summ and https://github.com/disi-unibo-nlp/carburacy.
